How to Use MokeSell

1. Browse Categories

Navigate through our various categories using the navigation menu at the top. Click on any category to view items specific to that category.

2. Search for Items

Use the search bar to find specific items you're interested in. You can filter results by price range and category.

3. Buy Now

When you find an item you want to purchase, click the "Buy Now" button to proceed with the transaction. Enter your shipping and payment details.

4. Wait for Your Item

After completing your purchase, wait for your item to arrive at the shipping address you provided.
